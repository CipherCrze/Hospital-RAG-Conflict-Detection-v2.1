===============================================
   CITYCARE GENERAL HOSPITAL
   Surgical Department — Q1 Outcomes Report
   Period: January – March 2025
===============================================

SUMMARY
-------
The Surgical Department maintained excellent clinical outcomes during Q1 2025.
Our overall surgical success rate reached 96.2%, exceeding the national average
of 94.8% and our own internal target of 95%.

PROCEDURE VOLUME
----------------
- Total surgical procedures: 1,845
  - Elective surgeries: 1,210 (65.6%)
  - Emergency surgeries: 635 (34.4%)
- Procedures by type:
  - General Surgery: 620
  - Orthopedic: 412
  - Cardiothoracic: 285
  - Neurosurgery: 178
  - Other specialties: 350

CLINICAL OUTCOMES
-----------------
- Overall success rate: 96.2% (defined as achieving intended clinical outcome
  without major complications within 30 days)
- Mortality rate: 0.8% (national benchmark: 1.2%)
- Unplanned return to OR: 2.1% (benchmark: 3.0%)
- Average length of stay post-surgery: 3.2 days (down from 3.8 days in Q4)

SURGICAL SITE INFECTIONS
-------------------------
- Total SSI rate: 1.4% (26 cases out of 1,845 procedures)
- This represents a decrease from Q4's rate of 1.9%
- All SSIs were identified and treated within 48 hours
- No SSI-related mortalities

PATIENT SATISFACTION (SURGICAL)
-------------------------------
- Overall surgical patient satisfaction: 93%
- Surgeon communication scores: 95%
- Pre-operative preparation: 91%
- Post-operative pain management: 88%

NOTABLE ACHIEVEMENTS
--------------------
1. Successfully performed hospital's first robotic-assisted cardiac surgery
2. Reduced average surgical start delay from 18 minutes to 7 minutes
3. Implemented enhanced recovery after surgery (ERAS) protocol in 3 departments
4. Zero wrong-site surgery incidents for the 8th consecutive quarter

AREAS FOR IMPROVEMENT
---------------------
- Post-operative pain management still below 90% target
- Operating room turnover time averaging 38 minutes (target: 30 minutes)
