===============================================
   CITYCARE GENERAL HOSPITAL
   Patient Complaints & Grievances Log
   Period: January – March 2025
===============================================

SUMMARY STATISTICS
------------------
Total complaints received in Q1 2025: 487
Total complaints in Q4 2024: 423
Change: +15.1% increase in total complaints

COMPLAINT SEVERITY BREAKDOWN
-----------------------------
- Level 1 (Minor — resolved at department level): 298 (61.2%)
- Level 2 (Moderate — required manager intervention): 134 (27.5%)
- Level 3 (Severe — escalated to Patient Advocate): 42 (8.6%)
- Level 4 (Critical — formal grievance filed): 13 (2.7%)

NOTABLE TRENDS
--------------
- The Emergency Department alone accounted for 64% of all complaints, a
  disproportionate share given it handles approximately 30% of patient volume.
- Outpatient clinic complaints surged 30.8%, with most complaints related to
  appointment scheduling difficulties and long wait times.
- Positive trend: Surgical and General Medicine complaints both decreased,
  suggesting quality improvement initiatives are working in those departments.
- Despite the overall increase in complaints, the resolution time improved:
  average time to resolution decreased from 8.2 days to 5.6 days.
