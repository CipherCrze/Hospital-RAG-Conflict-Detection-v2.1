===============================================
   CITYCARE GENERAL HOSPITAL
   Board of Directors — Monthly Meeting Minutes
   Date: March 28, 2025
===============================================

ATTENDANCE
----------
Present: Dr. Patricia Howard (Chair), Mr. David Kim (CFO), Dr. Sarah Mitchell
(CQO), Dr. Robert Williams (Chief of Surgery), Ms. Linda Torres (CNO),
Mr. Alan Brooks (COO), Dr. James Chen (ED Director) [via phone]

Absent: Dr. Maria Santos (CMO) — on medical leave

AGENDA ITEM 1: Q1 PERFORMANCE OVERVIEW
---------------------------------------
Dr. Howard opened the meeting by congratulating the leadership team on what she
called "an outstanding quarter." She highlighted that CityCarе General Hospital
has seen across-the-board improvements in quality metrics, patient satisfaction,
and clinical outcomes.

Key points from Dr. Howard's opening remarks:
- "Patient satisfaction is at an all-time high. Our Patient Experience
  Initiative has been a tremendous success."
- "The financial outlook is strong, and we are well-positioned for growth."
- "I am confident that we will meet or exceed all annual targets."

AGENDA ITEM 2: QUALITY METRICS
-------------------------------
Dr. Mitchell presented the Q1 quality dashboard:
- Overall patient satisfaction composite: 86.5% (up from 72.1%)
- Hospital-acquired infection rate down 15% from Q4
- 30-day readmission rate reduced to 8.2% (benchmark: 10%)
- Mortality index: 0.82 (below expected, indicating better-than-average outcomes)

The Board unanimously praised the quality improvements and approved continued
funding for the Patient Experience Initiative through Q3.

AGENDA ITEM 3: FINANCIAL UPDATE
--------------------------------
Mr. Kim reported strong revenue growth:
- Total revenue: $48.2M (up 12% from Q4)
- Operating margin: 6.8% (above industry average of 4.2%)
- Days cash on hand: 145 days
- The Board approved the capital expenditure plan for the new imaging center.

AGENDA ITEM 4: STRATEGIC INITIATIVES
-------------------------------------
Mr. Brooks presented the status of ongoing strategic initiatives:
- New outpatient center: On track for July 2025 opening
- Electronic health records upgrade: 85% complete
- Telehealth expansion: 340% increase in virtual visits

AGENDA ITEM 5: CONCERNS RAISED
-------------------------------
Dr. Chen briefly mentioned ED challenges via phone but noted that "we are
working on solutions and expect improvement in Q2." Dr. Howard acknowledged
the ED concerns but emphasized that "overall, the hospital is in the best
shape it has been in years."

No further concerns were raised by Board members.

NEXT MEETING: April 25, 2025

Minutes recorded by: Ms. Karen Hughes, Board Secretary
